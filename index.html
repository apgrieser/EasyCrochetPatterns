Welcome!

Discover easy and beautiful FINISHABLE crochet patterns for beginners and experts alike.

Please don't tell me I'm the only one who has had closets full of unfinished crochet projects. If a project is too easy, I might get bored with it. If a project is too difficult, then I might get frustrated and give up. I've been known to quit just because the finishing process required too many ends to be weaved in.

Of course, you may not have this problem at all. Or you may have a variation such as you do not mind some finishing work, but changing colors mid-row is intolerable. My mother used to call the intolerable type work a "potchke".

My crochet patterns are designed to be "finishable". To me, it can mean one or more things:

  • Very quick to create from start to finish.

  • Easy to find a stopping point from one crochet session to the next.

  • Easy to pick up again after pausing the project for a while.

  • Pattern is simple emough to work without having to constantly refer back to instructions.

  • Complexity level is enough to stay interested but not frustrated. This, of course, varies from person to person, so the patterns offer options.
